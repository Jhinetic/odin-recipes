Moroccan-Spanish Tortilla

How to Make Very Quick Moroccan-Spanish Tortilla

If you haven’t yet tasted or tried making Tortilla, let today be the day that you do.
This must-try Moroccan-Spanish dish is very easy to make.

Rabat- Morocco is very famous for its Spanish influence in food.
Tortilla is a Spanish-Moroccan Potato Omelette; a delicious and easy to make dish commonly served in Spanish and Moroccan homes.
Tortilla is also a popular street food in Morocco, where it might be eaten plain or used as a sandwich filler.

Ingredients

  • 4 large Potatoes

  • 1 medium Onion

  • 3 tablespoons of Olive oil

  • 5 of Eggs

  • 1/4 cup of Chopped parsley

  • 1 tablespoon of Paprika

  • 1/2 tablespoon of Cayenne (heat)

  • 1 tablespoon of Salt

  • 1 tablespoon of Black pepper

  • 1/2 tablespoon of Cumin powder.

  • Mayonnaise (optional)

  • Tomato sauce or Ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400° F.

  2. Remove the skins from the potatoes and slice thinly in rounds or quarter them. Boil until soft and drain off all water.

  3. In a medium size mixing bowl, whisk the eggs with the cream and salt. Add the boiled and drained potatoes.

  4. Using a large sauté pan, heat the olive oil.
    Add the whisked egg mixture and cook on medium-high until the bottom begins to brown. Place in the heated oven, uncovered.

  5. In a pan, add in 2 tablespoons of olive oil and the chopped onions along with salt.
    Fry for 5 minutes,then add in the potatoes and mix well.

  6. Add all the spices along with salt and pepper.
    Add in the remaining olive oil. On low heat, cover the pan and let the potatoes cook until tender.
    Bake the tortilla in a preheated oven at 180°C for about 25 minutes.

  7. When the tortilla is cooked, take it out of the oven and let it cool before unmolding it.

  8. Allow the tortilla to cool a few minutes before serving.

  9. Cut into triangular pieces and serve with mayonnaise and ketchup as optional toppings and rustic bread to eat as a sandwich, if desired.
    Just before serving, you can also decorate it with grated cheese or cherry tomatoes.

  10. Chehya tayba,Bon appétit.
